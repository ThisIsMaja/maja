Subscribe to Maja Talk

Welcome to Maja Talk, where we share what we usually don’t share, with you!

Each month we will open our hearts and our minds and let them spill into this newsletter. We want to share with you how our lifestyle really works and let you assess whether or not a life like ours is something you might want. For those of you who want to take a shot, we will try our best to give you the tools to achieve what we have. And for those of you who want something different we will share with you the tools that make us better humans everyday.

In this newsletter we will talk about business, we will talk about relationships, we will talk about our lows just as much as our highs, we will talk about health physical and mental, we will talk about the problems we face. We will talk about love, inspiration, society, self-improvement, sustainability and anything else that we believe might help you get to where you want to be. 

And the big difference between this newsletter and everything else you see us share with the world is that everything you read here will be UNFILTERED and RAW

Let’s grow together and drive each other to become the best version of ourselves through radical honesty and kindness. We can’t wait to get started.
