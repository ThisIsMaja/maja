Over the years we have learnt many lessons about life, love, travel, health and we’ve grown a lot because of these lessons. We’ve travelled the world and seen the most beautiful places, met the happiest people and discovered new ways of living that we didn’t know existed. Because of all this we’ve been able to collect the tools we needed to design a dream lifestyle for ourselves. A lifestyle that allows us to spend our time the way we want, gives us the opportunity to give back to others and the world, encourages us to make healthy choices , enables us to see the world and puts a smile on our faces everyday.

We’ve worked hard to make our dreams become a reality make no mistake about that, but the tools we’ve collected over the years, the lessons we’ve learnt and the thought processes we’ve constructed that have helped us succeed are very much acquirable. We want to share all this with you through our new blog. From how we stay healthy on the road to how we deal with the fear of failure. How we make money online to what products we use and love. We want this blog to be more than a travel blog, we want it to be an open book that we can all contribute to and learn from. A place that inspires creativity, positivity and love, a place you can come to when you need to remind yourself that anything is possible.
